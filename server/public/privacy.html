RideNow Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 22, 2026

RideNow ("the app") is operated by Daniel Sarmiento Cordero. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information we collect

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, and password (stored hashed).
  • Pickup and drop-off locations, and device location while requesting or taking a ride, used only to provide the ride service.
  • Ride history within the app.

How we use your information

Your information is used solely to provide the app's core functionality. We do not sell your personal data, share it with third parties for marketing, or serve advertising of any kind.

Data storage and security

Data is stored on our own servers (https://ridenow.champi.lat) and transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Passwords are stored using industry-standard hashing.

Third-party services

Map tiles and address search may be provided by OpenStreetMap-based services (CARTO, Nominatim). Requests to these services may include coarse location or the addresses you search for, subject to their own privacy policies.

Data retention and deletion

You may delete your account at any time from within the app, or request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing danielsarcor@gmail.com. Requests are honored within 30 days.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time; the latest version will always be available at this URL.

Contact
